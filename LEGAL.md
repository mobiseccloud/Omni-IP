# MASTER PRIVACY POLICY

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy outlines the privacy practices of Mobisec Cloud ("we", "us", "our") concerning the Omni-IP application ("Omni-IP", "the Application"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the highest level of security for our users. Omni-IP is engineered with a Zero-Backend Architecture, meaning privacy and data security are built into the foundational design of the Application.

## VpnService Utilisation
Omni-IP utilises the Android `VpnService` API to provide its core functionality. It is imperative to understand that this VpnService is used **exclusively** for local, on-device packet filtering, sinkholing, and generating local PCAP files for diagnostic purposes.
*   **No Remote Routing:** Network traffic intercepted by the VpnService is never routed to, transmitted through, or stored on any remote Mobisec servers. All packet inspection and filtering are executed locally on your device.

## Zero Data Harvesting
Mobisec Cloud adheres to a strict policy of Zero Data Harvesting.
*   We do not collect, track, monetise, or sell your browsing history, IP addresses, MAC addresses, network topographies, or any other metadata generated during your use of Omni-IP.
*   Any data generated by Omni-IP, including diagnostic logs, network scans, and PCAP files, remains strictly on your local device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## Third-Party APIs and Threat Intelligence
Omni-IP offers premium features that integrate with third-party threat intelligence services (e.g., AlienVault, AbuseIPDB, Shodan).
*   **Direct Device-to-Service Communication:** To utilise these features, you must provide your own API keys for the respective services. Omni-IP uses these keys to communicate directly from your device to the third-party service provider.
*   **Bypassing Our Infrastructure:** These threat intelligence queries entirely bypass Mobisec Cloud's infrastructure. We do not intermediate, log, or have visibility into the queries you make or the intelligence data you receive. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective third-party providers regarding their data handling practices.
